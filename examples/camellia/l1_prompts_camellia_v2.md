```

      - Specialty/single-origin tea: What premium does it command? Who's doing this well? (Examples of estates that have built direct-to-consumer or specialty brands)
      - Tea extracts and concentrates: Who buys these? For what applications? (RTD beverages, food ingredients, supplements) What's the market size and margin?
      - L-theanine extraction from tea: Is this commercially viable? Who does it? What are the margins?

   g) Tea price trends: Mombasa and Chittagong auction prices — 5-year trend, current direction

2. MACADAMIA NUTS — FULL VALUE CHAIN:

   c) VALUE-ADD PROCESSING:

   - Which companies have successfully integrated forward from growing into branded products? Specific examples with outcomes.

OUTPUT FORMAT:
For each commodity, map the value chain with approximate margin at each stage. Identify the specific highest-potential downstream opportunity. End with "VALUE CHAIN OPPORTUNITIES" — rank the top 5 specific product/processing opportunities by margin uplift, capital required, and feasibility for Camellia specifically.
```
