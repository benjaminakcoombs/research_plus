ROLE: You are a senior commodities research analyst specializing in lithium markets, covering both the physical market (supply, demand, inventories, cost curves) and the financial market (equity valuations, deal multiples, capital flows). Your clients include mining companies, battery manufacturers, automotive OEMs, and M&A-focused investment funds evaluating lithium sector deal timing. Your task is to produce the definitive lithium market outlook that underpins Albemarle's deal timing analysis.

CONTEXT: Lithium carbonate equivalent (LCE) prices peaked at approximately $80,000/MT in late 2022, crashed to approximately $9,655/MT at the trough, and have partially recovered to approximately $15,700/MT by late 2025. Global lithium demand surged over 30% in 2025, driven by 30% EV sales growth and 105% growth in grid storage installations, yet persistent oversupply from the 2021-2022 investment boom kept prices depressed. 40% of global lithium capacity is reportedly unprofitable at current prices, driving supply rationalization.

d) Map the pipeline of new lithium projects: which are under construction, which have received final investment decisions, and which have been deferred or cancelled? Estimate the supply additions by year (2026, 2027, 2028, 2029, 2030).
e) Research the Zimbabwe export ban (February 2026) — quantify the impact on global supply. Are other producing countries considering similar measures?
f) Research the Chinese lepidolite sector specifically — what percentage of Chinese lepidolite capacity has been shuttered? Is this permanent or temporary (price-responsive)?

2. DEMAND-SIDE ANALYSIS
a) Research global EV sales volumes and growth rates by region (China, Europe, North America, rest of world) for 2024, 2025, and consensus forecasts for 2026-2030.
b) The 105% growth in grid/utility-scale energy storage installations in 2025 is a potentially transformative demand signal. Research this in depth: what was the absolute volume (GWh deployed)? What lithium chemistry is being used (LFP vs. NMC)? What is the consensus growth forecast for grid storage through 2030? What does grid storage demand imply for total lithium consumption (express as % of total lithium demand)?
c) Research other lithium demand sources: consumer electronics, industrial applications, and any emerging use cases. Are these growing or declining?
d) Construct a total lithium demand model (MT LCE) for 2025, 2026, 2027, 2028, 2029, 2030 — broken down by EV, grid storage, and other.

3. SUPPLY-DEMAND BALANCE AND PRICE FORECAST
a) Compare your supply and demand projections to identify when the market transitions from surplus to deficit. Is the consensus view that the market rebalances in 2026, 2027, or later?
b) Research price forecasts from major banks and research houses: Goldman Sachs, Morgan Stanley, UBS, Citi, Macquarie, Benchmark Mineral Intelligence, Fastmarkets, S&P Global. What is the consensus price trajectory for LCE through 2030?
c) Construct three scenarios for lithium prices: Bear ($10,000-12,000/MT sustained), Base ($17,000-22,000/MT recovery), Bull ($25,000-35,000/MT supply crunch). For each scenario, describe the conditions that would produce it and estimate the probability.
d) Research lithium inventory levels at key storage points (China bonded warehouses, LME, producer inventories). Are inventories building, stable, or drawing down?

4. TECHNOLOGY DISRUPTION RISKS
a) Research Direct Lithium Extraction (DLE) technology: which companies are piloting or commercializing DLE? What is the realistic timeline for DLE to contribute meaningful new supply? Does Albemarle have its own DLE program?
b) Research sodium-ion battery technology: is it a credible substitute for lithium-ion in any major application (grid storage, low-end EVs)? What is the market consensus on sodium-ion's impact on lithium demand?

d) Research lithium recycling: what percentage of lithium demand could be met by recycled material by 2030? Who are the major recycling companies?

5. DEAL TIMING ANALYSIS — THE CORE OUTPUT
a) For a SELLER (Albemarle considering a sale): what is the optimal window? Should they sell now at trough (capturing certainty at a known price), wait for partial recovery (6-12 months?), or hold for a full cycle recovery ($20-25/kg)?

6. IMPLICATIONS FOR ALBEMARLE SPECIFICALLY
