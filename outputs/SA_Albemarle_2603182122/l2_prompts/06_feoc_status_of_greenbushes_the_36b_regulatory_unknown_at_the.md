The Inflation Reduction Act (IRA) Section 30D provides up to $7,500 in consumer EV tax credits, contingent on battery critical minerals NOT being "extracted, processed, or recycled by a foreign entity of concern" (FEOC). Covered nations include China, Russia, North Korea, and Iran.

Greenbushes — the world's largest hard-rock lithium mine — is operated by Talison Lithium, which is 51% controlled by TLEA. The TLEA ownership is: Tianqi Lithium (26.01%) and IGO Limited (24.99%). Tianqi Lithium Energy Australia (TLEA) is therefore a subsidiary with significant Chinese state-influenced ownership through Tianqi. Tianqi Lithium itself has Chinese state-connected investors in its shareholder base.

This statement links Albemarle's entire strategic thesis — and potentially $850M-$3.6B in enterprise value — to FEOC compliance of its supply chain.
