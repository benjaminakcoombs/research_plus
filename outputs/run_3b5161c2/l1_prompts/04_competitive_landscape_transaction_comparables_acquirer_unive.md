You are building a comprehensive buyer universe and competitive map for Acadia Healthcare Company, Inc. (NASDAQ: ACHC), the largest pure-play behavioral health provider in the United States with $3.313B in FY2025 revenue, 277 facilities, and 12,500+ licensed beds.

RESEARCH OBJECTIVE: Map the competitive landscape with granular operational comparisons, build a complete universe of potential acquirers (strategic and financial) with specific rationale for each, and compile every relevant M&A comparable transaction from the past 5 years.

D) CROSSROADS TREATMENT CENTERS (REVELSTOKE/CDPQ)

E) LIFESTANCE HEALTH GROUP (NASDAQ: LFST)

- How does LifeStance compete with Acadia? Is there patient flow between outpatient (LifeStance) and inpatient (Acadia)?
