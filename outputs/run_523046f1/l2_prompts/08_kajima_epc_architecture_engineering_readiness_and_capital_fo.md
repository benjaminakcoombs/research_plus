CONTEXT:

In project finance for industrial plants, the EPC (Engineering, Procurement, and Construction) contractor's creditworthiness is often the key to unlocking government debt financing. The DOD Office of Strategic Capital (OSC) requires a "repayment guarantee from a creditworthy sponsor" for pre-revenue companies seeking loans. An EPC performance guarantee from a $15B-revenue investment-grade contractor like Kajima would satisfy this requirement — potentially without needing a separate strategic co-investor guarantee from a cement major or aluminum company. This would be a structural shortcut: Kajima as both minority equity investor AND EPC contractor collapses the financing complexity.
