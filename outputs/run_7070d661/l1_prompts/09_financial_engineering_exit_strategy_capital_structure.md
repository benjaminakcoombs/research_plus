- Are there any pay-to-play, drag-along, or tag-along provisions?

- At what revenue does it reach free cash flow breakeven (including capex)?
- Is 2027 breakeven realistic, or is 2029+ more likely?
- Does the CEO's "never raise again" claim hold up under base case assumptions?
